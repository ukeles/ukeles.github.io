Umit Keles, Ph.D.

Hi, Welcome!

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the National Magnetic Resonance Research Center and the Sabuncu Brain Research Center at Bilkent University, Turkey. I work with Prof. Tolga Cukur as a member of the Imaging and Computational Neuroscience Laboratory.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Physics from Bilkent University in 2014. During my doctoral studies, I developed a computational framework for exploring the electronic structure and quantum transport properties of Si-based complex nanomaterials.

My current research focuses on how the human brain represents semantic information in natural visual and auditory scenes. By integrating data science and machine learning techniques with functional neuroimaging (fMRI) studies, this research aims to develop computational models that describe how the brain responds to specific features in stimuli.

I am especially interested in how the human brain processes information about the social world and how this processing interacts with social cognition and behavior.
